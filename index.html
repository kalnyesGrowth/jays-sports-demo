Questions

FAQ

Is Jay's Sports Lounge family-friendly?
Yes, all ages are welcome. Jay's is a great spot for family dinners, and children are welcome during regular hours. The nightlife atmosphere picks up later in the evening.
What are your happy hour specials?
Happy hour runs Monday through Saturday from 11:00 AM to 8:00 PM. Enjoy $3 for 22oz domestic drafts, $4 shots, and 25% off all appetizers.
Do you have outdoor seating?
Yes, we have a spacious outdoor patio area featuring a water fountain and comfortable seating. It's the perfect spot for warm weather dining and drinks.
How many beers do you have on tap?
We carry 60+ beers total with 32 always on draft. Our selection includes craft, domestic, imported, and local brews. The draft list rotates regularly so there's always something new to try.
Do you offer delivery?
Yes, we're available on DoorDash for delivery. You can order our full menu right to your door.
Do I need a reservation?
No reservation is needed for small groups. For large parties or special events, we recommend calling ahead at (540) 720-7000 to make arrangements.
Can I host a private event at Jay's?
Yes, we accommodate private parties and events. Contact us at info@jayssportslounge.com or call (540) 720-7000 to discuss your event needs.
What sports channels do you carry?
With 15+ large screen TVs and our JBL sound system, we show all major sporting events including NFL, NBA, MLB, UFC, college sports, and pay-per-view events.
